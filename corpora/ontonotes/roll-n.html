roll-n; 15 Senses

Sense Number 1: the rotating motion of an object

Commentary: ROLL[+event][+motion][+rotary]

Examples:
The jet began its roll down the runway. (wheels are rotating)
The dog enjoyed a long roll in the grass.
We practiced forward rolls in gymnastics today.
Mary gave the bowling ball a roll that sent it down the center of the lane.
He sensed Sally's annoyance by the roll of her eyes. (metaphoric extension)

Mappings:
PropBank: roll.01
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 15

Sense Number 2: a roster of names

Commentary: ROLL[+artifact][+document][+roster][+of_names]
NOTE: contrast this to Sense 14, which refers to the people on the roster
