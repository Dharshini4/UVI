statistic-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: datum

Commentary: a datum that can be represented numerically

Examples:
Statistics show that educated women are getting married later in life.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: person

Commentary: reference to a person as though they were a piece of statistical information

Examples:
If we don't do something, that girl will become a statistic.
