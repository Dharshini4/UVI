allocate-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: distribute or set aside according to plan

Examples:
I was allocated a Daily Express cruise.
They allocated lots of space lately but not much of that is yet in use.
He will allocate a room to be used for storage.
I will allocate rations for a week-long camping trip.
I was allocated to the Department of Environmental Planning.
Ministry of Agriculture allocated rations during WWII.
