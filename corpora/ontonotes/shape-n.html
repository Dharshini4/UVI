shape-n; 5 Senses

Sense Number 1: the spatial, geometric contour of an object

Commentary: SHAPE[+form][+contour][+spatial][+of_object]

Examples:
She admired the shape of the Grecian urn.
A large shape loomed ahead in the fog, floating on the water.
The building blocks came in an assortment of shapes.
He thought he saw a shape moving through the trees.
