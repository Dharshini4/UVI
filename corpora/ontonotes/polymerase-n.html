polymerase-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: an enzyme that brings about the formation of a particular polymer, e.g, DNA

Commentary: POLYMERASE[+substance][+chemical][+enzyme][+agent][+for_polymer][+formation]

Examples:
A DNA polymerase assists in the replication of DNA.
Transcription of the genetic information in all cells is carried out by multisubunit RNA polymerases.
