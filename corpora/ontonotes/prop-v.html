prop-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: support by placing on or against something solid

Commentary: Includes: PROP UP, PROP OPEN

Examples:
We just propped the camera on a box and let it roll for this one.
We propped the newly cut plywood table on studio wall.
I sat on Jack's porch while he propped the aluminum door open with his boot.
I propped the antenna up on the floor next to my laptop.
