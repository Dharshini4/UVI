premiere-v; 1 Senses

Examples:
"Mission Impossible" premiered on television in 1967.
On September 5, the sequel will premiere in Scandinavia, Turkey, South Africa, and Poland.
Macferron premiered the work on November 5, 1995 in Seattle.
Who premiered the violin concerto by Karl Goldmark?
