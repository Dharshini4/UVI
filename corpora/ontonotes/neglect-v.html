neglect-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: leave undone or leave out

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1 NEGLECT [predicate]

Examples:
She neglected to say what it was for.
They neglected to get our oil supplies and our submarines.
They will rue the day they neglected to read my mail.

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1 NEGLECT NP2

Examples:
He neglected his health for years.
He neglects the prayer which is the pillar of religion.
I would never neglect my children!
If they neglected their duty, they were severely punished.
