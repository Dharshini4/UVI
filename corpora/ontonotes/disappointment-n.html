disappointment-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: a feeling of dissatisfaction that results when one's expectations are not realized, an act (or failure to act) that disappoints someone

Commentary: DISAPPOINTMENT[+event][+human][+thing_which_disappoints]

Examples:
His hopes were so high he was doomed to disappointment.
What a disappointment!
Disappointment is a common feeling after a stressful interview.
