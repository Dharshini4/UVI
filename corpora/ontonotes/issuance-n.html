issuance-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: something being issued

Commentary: ISSUANCE[+event] The act of sending something out, usually in large quantities.

Examples:
Yesterday I ordered the issuance of a new set of stamps.
Congress passed regulations regarding the issuance of passports to minors.
