passenger-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: a traveler in a conveyance or vehicle, and who does not operate the conveyance

Commentary: PASSENGER[+person][+traveler][+by_conveyance][-operator]

Examples:
The bus discharged all the passengers at the terminal.
He was a stowaway, a hidden passenger in the cargo hold.
The passenger next to the driver was injured.
