crow-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: A human expresses pleasure verbally, perhaps gloating.

Examples:
She crowed with joy.
He can't stop crowing about his good marks in school.

Examples:
The cocks crowed all morning.
Certain birds crow as a warning.
