leach-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: (Cause to) percolate or permeate through something.

Commentary:
NOTE: Usually LEACH INTO

Examples:
Toxins leached into the soil.
The fertilizer leached into the water supply.

Mappings:

FrameNet: NP
PropBank: NM

Sense Number 2: (Cause to) lose substances from something.

Examples:
Farming leaches the soil of nutrients.
Carbonation is said to leach calcium from the bones.
