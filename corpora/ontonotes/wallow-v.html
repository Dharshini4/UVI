wallow-v; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: indulge in, usually with pleasure

Commentary: Syntax Includes:

Examples:
Jeanie wallowed in self-pity after her break up.
Francis wallowed in pleasure during her massage.
Stockbrokers wallow in delight when their investments go up.
Jermaine wallowed in the success of his party.

Sense Number 2: roll around

Examples:
Pigs wallow in the mud to cool off.
Dogs wallow in garbage to cover their scent.
Sharon wallowed in the gigantic bathtub.

Sense Number 3: rise up as if in waves

Examples:

Steam wallowed from the boiling pot.
Dust wallowed around their feet as they walked through the desert.
