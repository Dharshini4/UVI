place-vpc-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: PLACE DOWN-cause to sit; be settled in a position or place

Commentary: Is: PLACE DOWN

Examples:
Pick the cup up again, then place it down so the gaff is dislodged.
Place the menu down the side of your web pages.

Commentary: Is: PLACE UPRIGHT
