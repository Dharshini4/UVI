spray-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: (Cause to) discharge in a mass or jet of droplets, perhaps in order to cover. May be figurative.

Examples:
Water sprayed all over the floor.
Please spray paint on that far wall.
The oil company claims that they were not allowed to spray dispersant
on the spill.
Don't spray water on your sister!
The company sprayed the internet with ads promoting its new product.

Sense Number 2: Mark with urine, as an animal might.

Examples:
Lions spray their territory.
My cat sprayed all over the new house.
Neutered dogs are less likely to spray.
