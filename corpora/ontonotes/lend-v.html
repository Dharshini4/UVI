lend-v; 4 Senses

Sense Number 1: bestow a particular quality

Commentary: Syntax Includes: NP1 LEND NP2[attribute] PP{to NP} NP1 LEND NP3 NP2[attribute]

Examples:
Vases of flowers all around the room lent the place a cheerful look.
These events lend support to the view that the law is inadequate.
Her presence lends a certain cachet to the company.

Mappings:
VerbNet: give-13.1
FrameNet: NP
PropBank: NM
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: give something temporarily expecting it to be returned

Commentary: Syntax Includes: NP1 LEND NP2[patient] (PP{to NP}) NP1 LEND (NP3) NP2[patient]

Examples:
She never lends her books.
The bank refuses to lend to students.
If you need a coat I can lend you one.
The bank agreed to lend him $5000.

Mappings:
VerbNet: NM
FrameNet: NP
PropBank: lend.01

The current system lends (itself) to great abuse.
