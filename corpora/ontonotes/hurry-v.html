hurry-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Move oneself very fast.

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1[agent] HURRY
NOTE: May have a reflexive NP2.

Examples:
We must hurry!
I'm hurrying to class.
The president hurried to sign the bill.
The children hurry home for lunch.

Mappings:
VerbNet: rush-53.2,run-51.3.2-2
FrameNet: Self_motion
PropBank: hurry.01
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 2

Sense Number 2: Rush someone or something else.

Commentary: Includes: HURRY ALONG

Examples:
The president hurried the bill along.
Hurry your sister - we don't have all day!
Don't hurry me!

Mappings:
VerbNet: rush-53.2
FrameNet: NM
PropBank: NM
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 3
