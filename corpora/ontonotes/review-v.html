review-v; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: examine, inspect, study or consider critically, again

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1[agent] REVIEW NP2

Examples:
The committee is reviewing the current situation.
Let's review what has happened so far.
He reviewed his options before making a final decision.
They need to review their sales strategy.
The Queen reviewed the troops on her recent visit.
Now that you have reviewed the evidence, take some time to analyze its importance.
I reviewed the material before the test.
Be sure to review the margin notes and boxed comments for major concepts.

Mappings:
VerbNet: NM
FrameNet: NP
PropBank: review.01
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 3, 4

Sense Number 2: apprise critically, give opinion

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1[agent] REVIEW NP2
NOTE: NP2 is usually a form of media: play, book, movie, concert, or other public performances.

Examples:
She reviews books for the New York Times
He reviews movies for a newspaper.
I only go to see films that are reviewed favorably.

Mappings:
VerbNet: assessment-34.1
FrameNet: NP
PropBank: NM
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2

Sense Number 3: look back in retrospect, remember

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1 REVIEW NP2
NOTE: NP2 is usually refers to history or past events.

Examples:
She's writing an article reviewing the company's history.
She reviewed her achievements with pride.
