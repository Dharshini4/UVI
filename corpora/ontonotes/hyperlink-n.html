hyperlink-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: a connection, pointer, established via software between a hypertext and some other document

Commentary: HYPERLINK[+information][+software][+pointer][+from_hypertext][+to_document]

Examples:
The wikipedia article had a number of hyperlinks to related topics.
The blog site has a hyperlink to that recent article from Nature.
