lean-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: having a tilted orientation in space, relative to ground

Commentary: LEAN[+quality][+spatial][+orientation][+tilted]

Examples:
The tower has a distinct leftward lean to it.
The boat's lean or heel is counterbalanced by its keel.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: meat without fat in it

Commentary: LEAN[+quality][+flesh][+animal][+without_fat]

Examples:
John will only eat the lean cuts.
Mary ordered a pound of the lean and a pound of the chuck.
