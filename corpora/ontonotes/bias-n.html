bias-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: A partiality that prevents objective consideration of an issue or situation.

Commentary: BIAS[+abstract]

Examples:
She has a bias concerning her efficiency as a worker.
We cannot approach the law with bias.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: A line or cut across a fabric that is not at right angles to a side of the fabric.

Commentary: BIAS[+concrete]

Examples:
