troop-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: group

Commentary: an orderly crowd

Examples:
A troop of children crossed the street just as the light turned yellow.
Troop number 47 sold the most cookies.
A whole troop of people lined up behind me before I could decide which sandwich to buy.
