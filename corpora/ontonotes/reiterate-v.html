reiterate-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: say, state, or perform again

Examples:
She reiterated UNICEF's call on governments to abide by their commitments.
They reiterated the need for enhancing public awareness and confidence in biotechnology.
He reiterated our view that he expressed a year ago.
Without the agrarian reform, he reiterated, it is impossible to solve the current problems.
