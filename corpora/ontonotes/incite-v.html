incite-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: Give an incentive for action, provoke or stir up, urge on, or cause to act.

Examples:
Their actions incited a riot.
This incited me to sacrifice my career.
The High Court found him in contempt of court for inciting illegal
occupations of white-owned farms.
