plummet-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: drop or fall sharply

Examples:
Market share may have plummeted.
Support for the US-led war on terror has plummeted in Spain.
The Asbury Park Press plummeted after Gannett bought it from independent ownership.
He plummeted from the airplane.
