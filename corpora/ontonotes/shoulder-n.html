shoulder-n; 4 Senses

Sense Number 1: The part of the body between the neck and upper arm, or any of its subparts.

Commentary: SHOULDER[+body_part]

Examples:
I hurt my shoulder playing basketball.
She has very strong shoulders.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 3

Sense Number 2: A cut of meat including the upper joint of the foreleg.

Commentary: SHOULDER[+food]

Examples:
I got some pork shoulder for dinner.
The shoulder is a good cut of meat.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2

Sense Number 3: The part of a garment that covers or fits over the shoulder.

Commentary: SHOULDER[+clothing]

Examples:
A gold braid lay over the shoulder of his uniform.
We no longer wear padded shoulders in our outfits.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 4

Sense Number 4: A narrow edge of land (usually unpaved) along the side of a road.

Commentary: SHOULDER[+transportation]

Examples:
The car pulled off onto the shoulder.
This road has very narrow shoulders.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 5
