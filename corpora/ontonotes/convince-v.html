convince-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: persuade or be persuaded of a belief or course of action

Examples:
He managed to convince the jury of his innocence.
It's useless trying to convince her that she doesn't need to lose any weight.
I hope this will convince you to change your mind.
We are convinced of his guilt.
Nothing would convince them to invest in such a scheme.
