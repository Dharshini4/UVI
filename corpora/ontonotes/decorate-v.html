decorate-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: add ornaments to make something more attractive

Examples:
We decorated our house for the party.
Flowers and memorabilia decorated every shelf.
Jean decorates gifts baskets for a living.
Please decorate the wedding cake with pink roses.
