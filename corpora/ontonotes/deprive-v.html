deprive-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: take away or keep from having

Examples:
Their gaming, night after night, deprived him of sleep.
The petitioner has not shown that the Court deprived him of a constitutional right.
Her sprained ankle deprived her of mobility.
He is sentenced to 12 years' imprisonment and deprived his political rights for 4 years.
