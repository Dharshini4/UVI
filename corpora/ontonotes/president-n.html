president-n; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: chair

Commentary: chairperson, chairman, chairwoman, prexy - the executive officer of a firm, corporation, or university

Examples:
The president realized that the company was going bankrupt.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 1 4 5

Sense Number 2: head of a country

Commentary: the chief executive of a republic

Examples:
After the exportation of Captain Hook, Peter Pan was elected President of Neverland.
Canaan Banana was Zimbabwe's first black president.

Mappings:

Sense Number 3: head of U.S.

Commentary: President of the United States, United States President, President, Chief Executive - the office of the United States head of state

Examples:
A President is elected every four years.
The President likes to job every morning.
