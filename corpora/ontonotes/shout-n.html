shout-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: a loud cry or emphatic utterance

Commentary: SHOUT[+event][+speech_act][+utterance][+loud][+emphatic]

Examples:
He could hear the shouts of the angry mob gathered below his window.
The crowd gave a shout of joy as the baseball flew over the fence for a home run.
Bob gave a shout of warning as the tree began to topple over.
