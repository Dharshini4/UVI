redemption-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: (theology) the act of deliving from sin or saving from evil.

Commentary: REDEMPTION[+event][+theology]

Examples:
Redemption comes only from Christ.
In Judaism, redemption is a personal act with the Almighty.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: The act of purchasing back something previously sold.

Commentary: REDEMPTION[+event}[+financial]

Examples:
The company achieved redemption of its stock yesterday.
Redemption is a difficult process for mid-range companies.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2, 3
