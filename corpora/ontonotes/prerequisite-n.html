prerequisite-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: a prior condition or completed state that is required to achieve something

Commentary: PREREQUISITE[+condition][+prior][+required][+to_achieve][+goal]

Examples:
The prerequisites for this course include integral calculus and probability theory.
Sally lacks the prerequisite of patience to be a good gardener.
