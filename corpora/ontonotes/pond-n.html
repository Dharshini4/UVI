pond-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: a small lake, small body of fresh water

Commentary: POND[+body][+water][+fresh][+lake][+small]

Examples:
We went fishing at the pond on farmer Brown's land.
Mosquitoes lay their eggs in ponds and other small bodies of standing water.
They discovered elevated levels of chemical runoff in the local ponds and streams.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: nickname for Atlantic Ocean, somewhat facetious

Commentary: POND[+body][+water][+salt][+large][+nickname]

Examples:
His music is relatively unknown this side of the pond.
We're just going across the pond to catch some shows in London.
