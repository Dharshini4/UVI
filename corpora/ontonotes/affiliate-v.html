affiliate-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: join or be in an affiliation; closely associate

Examples:
That station is affiliated with Fox.
Politically he affiliates with the Democratic party.
He is an imbecile, so is the rest of the gang that he affiliates himself with.
