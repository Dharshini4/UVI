nobble-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Deprive of something through theft or deceit.

Examples:
He nobbled me out of my savings.
She nobbled the trusting client.
The cashier nobbled me by giving insufficient change.
Kidnappers nobbled their son.

Mappings:

PropBank: nobble.01
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 2, 3

Sense Number 2: Disable by drugging, as of an animal.

Examples:
He nobbled all the race horses before the Downs.
I nobbled a greyhound to eliminate the competition.
