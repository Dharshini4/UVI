inspissate-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: make or become thick

Examples:
This, the Bayenga inspissate by boiling.
The juices inspissate.
The eggs remain exposed to the sun till the oily part has time to inspissate.
Inspissate the tar so that it becomes pitch.
The egg yolk will inspissate.
Inspissate it by adding flour.
