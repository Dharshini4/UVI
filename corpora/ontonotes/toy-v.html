toy-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: Manipulate manually or in one's mind or imagination, possibly in a playful way.

Examples:
She toyed with her wedding ring.
It's cruel the way Mary toys with John's emotions.
He toyed with the idea of running for the Senate.
He shouldn't toy with a young girl's affections.
