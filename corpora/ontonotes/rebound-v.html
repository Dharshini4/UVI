rebound-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: bounce, recoil

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1[topic] REBOUND PP[locative]

Examples:
The basketball rebounded from the rim of the hoop.
His strong-arm tactics have rebounded on him. (figurative, backfired)
He hit a long drive out to center field, it hit the wall and rebounded for a double.

Mappings:
PropBank: rebound.01
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: recover, return to former state

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1[topic] REBOUND PP

Examples:
Stocks rebounded on strong earnings reports today.
John hasn't fully rebounded from his breakup with Betty.
The child rebounded quickly after his surgery.
