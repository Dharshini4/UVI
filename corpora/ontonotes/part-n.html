part-n; 7 Senses

Sense Number 1: portion

Commentary: component part, component, piece - something determined in relation to something that includes it

Examples:
He wanted to feel a part of something bigger than himself.
I read a part of the manuscript.
The smaller part is hard to reach.
They analyzed the river into three parts.
The rear part of the house.
Glue the two parts together.
Religions is in all parts of the world.
The written part of the exam.
The finance section of the company.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 1 2 4 5 7

Sense Number 2: our/his/my part

Commentary: so far as concerns the actor specified (can be replaced with "by him/us/my")

Examples:
It requires vigilance on our part.
They resisted every effort on his part.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 3

Sense Number 3: function

Commentary: office, role - the actions and activities assigned to or required or expected of a person or group

Examples:
The part of a teacher is not to act as a babysitter.
The government must do its part.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 6

Sense Number 4: theatrical role

Commentary: character, role, persona - an actor's portrayal of someone in a play

Examples:
She played the part of Desdemona.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 8

Sense Number 5: share

Commentary: portion - assets belonging to or due to or contributed by an individual person or group

Examples:
He wanted his part in cash.
They all did their part of the work.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 9 10

Sense Number 6: voice

Commentary: the melody carried by a particular voice or instrument in polyphonic music

Examples:
He tried to sing the tenor part.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 11

Sense Number 7: divide in hair

Commentary: a line where the hair is parted

Examples:
His part was right in the middle.
She moved her part to the side when she went out at night.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 12
