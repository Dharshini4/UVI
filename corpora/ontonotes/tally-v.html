tally-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: gain points in a game; add up (numbers)

Commentary: Syntax includes: NP1[agent] TALLY (up) NP2 Includes: TALLY UP
NOTE: The numeric value of NP2 is usually implied as in Examples 1, 2 and 4: goal, votes, list of guests and not obviously stated as in Example 3: values.

Examples:
Blake Gallagher tallied the only goal of the second period.
I tallied the complete list of party guests.
Tally up all the values in Column A.
After the city tallies up the votes, the announcement is made.

Sense Number 2: be compatible, similar or consistent

Commentary: Syntax is: NP1[theme{plural}] TALLY NP1[theme] TALLY with NP2[theme]

Examples:
These two stories don't seem to tally.
The fingerprints on the gun tally with those of the suspect.
This OS does not tally with the newest World of Warcraft version.
