fruit-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: The ripened reproductive body of a seed plant.

Commentary: FRUIT[+plant]

Examples:
I'll set some fruit out for snack.
We always have a big bowl of fruit around.
Fruit hung from the limbs of the tree.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: The yield or consequence of some action or process.

Commentary: FRUIT[+result]

Examples:
Bless the fruit of your womb, Jesus.
He lived long enough to see the fruit of his policies.
We will bear great fruits from this endeavor.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2, 3
