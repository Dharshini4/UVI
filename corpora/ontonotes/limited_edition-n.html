limited_edition-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: a reproduction of something for which there are only a numbered set of copies

Commentary: LIMITED_EDITION[+artifact][+reproduction][+collection][+restricted][+numbered]

Examples:
The Franklin mint has made only a limited edition of this commemorative coin.
Jane bought a limited edition of Shelley's poetry.
This engraving is one of a limited edition from a master woodblock of Rembrandt.
