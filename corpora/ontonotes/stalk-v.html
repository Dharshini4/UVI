stalk-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: follow stealthily, hunt

Commentary: Syntax is: NP1[agent] STALK (NP2) (FOR) NP3

Examples:

He knew Dee Borah would track him down like a cat stalking the
wilds for food.
The girl said he stalked her for years with sexually violent
messages before pushing his way into her house.
