refocus-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: change or renew focus

Examples:
During his five-year tenure in this position he refocused the company on respiratory and allergy medicines.
She refocused, caught a bit of movement in the den, but it was only the old dog.
He refocused his gaze upon the teacher.
