indulge-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: Allow free rein to enjoyment

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1 INDULGE (in) (NP2)

Examples:
The writer indulged in metaphorical language.
Throughout the lecture, he indulged in obscene gestures that left the
audience baffled.
Don't indulge the students' behavior!
Grandparents often indulge the children over holiday periods.
Don't indulge the students.
She indulges in ice cream sometimes three times a day.
Those who often indulged in fatty meats did not have a significantly
higher risk of prostate cancer.
