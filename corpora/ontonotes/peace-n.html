peace-n; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: not fighting

Commentary: harmonious relations, freedom from disputes - the state prevailing during the absence of war

Examples:
The roommates lived together in peace.
After six months in the woods George found himself at peace with nature.

Mappings:
PropBank: NM
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 1 2 5

Sense Number 2: serenity

Commentary: peacefulness, peace of mind, repose, heartease, ataraxis - the absence of mental stress or anxiety

Examples:
Her mind was at peace when she learned her husband was okay.
The redundant nature of the phrase "Rest In Peace" made Sally chuckle during the funeral.

Mappings:
PropBank: NM
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 3

Sense Number 3: public wellbeing

Commentary: the general security of public places (used almost exclusively in the phrase "disturbing the peace")

Examples:
He was arrested for disturbing the peace.
