vacation-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Leisure time away from work devoted to rest or pleasure.

Commentary: VACATION[+leisure][+event]

Examples:
We take a vacation at the end of July.
He had a short vacation in Puerto Rico.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: The act of making something legally void.

Commentary: VACATION[+law][+event]

Examples:
The vacation of his sentence left his speechless with joy.
The defense urged a vacation of all charges.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2
