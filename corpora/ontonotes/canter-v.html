canter-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: Go or ride at a canter.

Examples:
Austin kicked his horse into a canter
She stretched her weary legs into a canter, then a lope.
That was as fast as a canter, as smooth as a running walk.
He put his horse at a sharp canter, and started round the path.
Sometimes he breaks into a canter.
