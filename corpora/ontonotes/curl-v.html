curl-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: (cause to) form, shape, or twist into curls

Commentary: Includes: CURL UP Syntax Is: NP1[agent] CURL (UP) (NP2[patient]) NP1[theme] CURL

Examples:
He curled up by Mother Bear to get warm, and he tried to go back to
sleep, but he was worried.
She curled her hair by dipping a hairbrush in water and twirling
sections of hair around a rung from a discarded chair.
A fire crackled cheerily and blue smoke curled upward and a savory
odor of hot coffee hung in the air.
The road curled around open fields overgrown with weeds, up onto a
hill that gave a beautiful panoramic view of the main highway that
passes by Jarinu.
