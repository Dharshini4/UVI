sunday-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: the day of the week that comes after saturday and before monday

Commentary: SUNDAY[+time][+day][+of_week][+between_saturday_and_monday]

Examples:
The company picnic will be next sunday.
Every sunday you see families picnicking in the park.
