rejuvenate-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: (Cause to) assume the topographical features of a previous time.

Examples:
The land rejuvenated.
Engineers rejuvenated the mountainside.
The riven rejuvenated itself.

Sense Number 2: (Cause to) become more youthful or assume a fresher appearance or outlook.

Examples:
The week at the spa rejuvenated her.
The contact with his family rejuvenates him.
He rejuvenated when he became a grandfather.
