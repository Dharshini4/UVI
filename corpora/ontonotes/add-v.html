add-v; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: increase, supplement, enhance, constitute an addition

Commentary:
NOTE: Throughout this task, please beware of adjectival usages, such as "added value"(adj+noun)--but note that "added value" may also be a verb+object (sense 3). Please decide carefully, and tag "X" for adjectives.
NOTE: Compare carefully to sense 3; in this sense, a unit is given to an entity, a portion is given to a total. In sense 3, parts 1 and 2 are combined to create the entity (a number or total). Syntax Includes: NP1[agent] ADD (NP2[theme]) (PP{to NP3[target]}) In stocks: NP1[financial institution] ADD NP2[numerical value] to NP3[total worth]

Examples:

We are going to add a new sun deck to our house.

This adds a light note to the program.
This paper will add to her reputation.
Live piano jazz as well as selected background music adds to the experience.
