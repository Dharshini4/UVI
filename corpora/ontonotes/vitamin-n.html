vitamin-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: organic compound required as nutrient by an organism and not able to be synthesized by the organism

Commentary: VITAMIN[+substance][+compound][+organic][+nutrient][+required][-synthesized][+by_organism]

Examples:
Fresh vegetable and fruits contain important vitamins for good health.
It is possible to accumulate too much of a fat-soluble vitamin in the body.
