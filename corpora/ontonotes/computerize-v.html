computerize-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: shift a process, a function, or data storage to computers

Examples:
After they computerized their shipping outlets, two things happened.
They computerized the crocodiles in the Lion King for no apparent reason, as they looked fine in the original version.
Have they computerized more of the process, or does it involve a lot of manual calculations?
They computerized their order processing.
The hospital will computerize by the end of the year.
In just 10 days we will computerize your paper-based Trading System.
Physicians should computerize writing perscriptions and storing patient records.
