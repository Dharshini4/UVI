assess-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: judge or decide the amount, value, quality or importance

Examples:
The insurers will need to assess the flood damage.
They assessed the cost of the flood damage at 1500.
Examinations are not the only means of assessing a student's ability.
It's too early to assess the long-term consequences.
We need to assess whether the project is worth doing.
