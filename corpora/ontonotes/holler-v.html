holler-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: utter a sudden cry; complain

Commentary: Includes: HOLLER AT

Examples:

What are you hollering about?
The loggers operating the rafts hollered back and forth to one
another.
He hollered out to us to surrender our weapons.
