stink-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Be extremely bad in quality or performance, from the point of view of someone perceiving it.

Examples:

Sense Number 2: (Cause to) smell bad.

Commentary: Includes: STINK OUT, STINK UP

Examples:

Ralph always stinks the place up with his horrid body odor.
