discuss-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: talk about, consider, or examine in detail

Examples:
We discussed our household budget.
The police want to discuss neighborhood saftey with the local people.
The article discussed the different aspects of the posed question.
The later chapters discuss the effects on the environment.
