discount-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: a reduction in price, a deduction

Commentary: DISCOUNT[+quantity][+money][+deduction][+refund/+allowance]

Examples:
They are selling all the merchandise in the store at discount.
He manages a chain of discount stores.
You'll get a 10 percent discount on the retail price if you send in that rebate.
As a tailor, my uncle gets a trade discount on many of his fabrics.
There were no good discounts on consumer electronics at that store.

Sense Number 2: amount of interest charged for borrowing money

Commentary: DISCOUNT[+quantity][+money][+interest][+loan]

Examples:
The Federal Reserve set a discount rate of 4.5 percent to its member banks.
What is the discount on this savings bond?
