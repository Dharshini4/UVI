repeat-n; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: a recurring event

Commentary: REPEAT[+event][+recurrence]

Examples:
This television episode is a repeat from last season.
John wants to avoid a repeat of his argument with Mary yesterday.
The student's repeat of the English class was necessary because he'd flunked it the first time.
The disc jockey played Oldies for two hours without any repeats.
We're having a repeat of last night's dinner - beer and pizza.
