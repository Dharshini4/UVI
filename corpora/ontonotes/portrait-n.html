portrait-n; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: a painting or photograph that is a likeness of (usually) a person

Commentary: PORTRAIT[+entity][+artifact][+representation][+visual][+picture][+likeness][+of_individual]

Examples:
This Spanish artist painted the official portraits of all the members of the royal family.
Mary had a portrait made of each of her three children.
That street artist specializes in pastel portraits of people's pets.
My grandfather's portrait was done in oil on canvas.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: a portrayal of someone or something, through words or behavior

Commentary: PORTRAIT[+entity][+symbolic][+information][+characterization][+encompassing] [+activity][+process][+communication][+portrayal]

Examples:
The writer offers a fascinating portrait of the small fishing community.
His portrait of the villain in the 1998 remake of the film has a postmodern slant.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2
