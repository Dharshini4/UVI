revamp-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: renovate, repair, or revise

Examples:
The student loan program has been completely revamped.
You need to revamp the final section of this book.
Kmart has revamped the pharmacy department as a destination in its own right.

Mappings:
VerbNet: NP
FrameNet: NP
PropBank: revamp.01
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: provide (a shoe) with a new vamp

Commentary: This is a mistake in Wordnet. To vamp a shoe means to provide it with a new vamp. Vamp was also used to mean renovate or repair. According to the OED, revamp was never used in this way.
