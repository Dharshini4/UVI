envision-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: Conceive as a mental representation or projection, either of something real or hypothetical.

Examples:
I can't envision Bob ever riding horses.
I can envision exactly what will happen.
I cannot envision him as President.
She can't envision much beyond herself.
The company envisions capital expenses of about $180 million a year.
