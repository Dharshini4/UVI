re-create-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Create anew; do over.

Examples:
We re-created the economic boom on a small scale.
He re-creates famous pictures.
Re-create that drawing for practice.

Sense Number 2: Make a mental replica of.

Commentary:
NOTE: creation must be mental only.

Examples:
His mind re-creates the entire world.
I have re-created my life in my imagination.
