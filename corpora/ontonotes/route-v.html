route-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: Send or divert via a specific path or to specific destinations, perhaps of documents or electricity.

Examples:
The side car routes program trades into a special computer file that
scans for imbalances of buy and sell orders.
Orders were routed and executed in Los Angeles.
Route the low voltage to the engine cylinders.
