skitter-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Hurriedly glide or skip along

Examples:
Terrified, they skittered to higher ground.
The stone skittered across the pond.
The crab skittered sideways across the beach.
The scared child skittered off to find his mother.
The Frisbee skittered across the ground, just waiting for the dog.
