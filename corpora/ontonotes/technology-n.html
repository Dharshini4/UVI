technology-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: applied science

Commentary: The practical application of science to society and everyday life.

Examples:
Modern technology is so difficult to keep up with.
Before you get your new computer home the technology will be outdated.

Mappings:
WordNet 1.7 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: the discipline

Commentary: The study of the practical application of science to modern life.

Examples:
I guess he's studying technology over at that new technical school.
He had trouble deciding which branch of engineering to study.

WordNet 1.7 Sense Numbers: 2
