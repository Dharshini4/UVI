stature-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: the prestige or regard that someone possesses

Commentary: STATURE[+relation][+social][+rank][+prestige]

Examples:
He is an architect of international stature.
These public scandals have diminished the film star's stature.
They are a bunch of losers with low stature in their community.

Sense Number 2: a person's (or animal's) natural height

Commentary: STATURE[+quality][+height][+natural][+individual][+person/+animal] [+quantity][+measure][+approximate][+height]

Examples:
Basketball players are usually of very tall stature.
