insert-n; 4 Senses

Sense Number 1: a loose page tucked into a publication

Commentary: INSERT[+artifact][+section][+document][+page][+loose][+in_publication]

Examples:
Mary removed all the inserts from her new magazine.
The Sunday newspaper is full of ad and coupon inserts.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: an ornamental panel or section of a plain garment

Commentary: INSERT[+artifact][+section][+garment][+panel][+ornamental]

Examples:
The denim jacket and two beautiful needlepoint inserts on the front.
The throw pillows had tooled leather inserts on one side.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2

Sense Number 3: a local announcement made within a public broadcast

Commentary: INSERT[+event][+announcement][+local][+in_broadcast][+network]

Examples:
The local congressman paid for an insert during nationally broadcast baseball game.
The inserts for a local furniture store are broadcast only locally during the airing of that T.V. show.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 3

Sense Number 4: a shot inserted into a film or video

Commentary: INSERT[+artifact][+section][+media][+movie][+shot][+cut_in]

Examples:
An insert in the film announced that ten years had gone by.
An old photo of the artist was placed as an insert in the documentary about him.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 4
