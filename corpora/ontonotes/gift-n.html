gift-n; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: Something acquired without compensation.

Commentary: GIFT[+concrete]

Examples:
She got many gifts at the holidays.
Chocolate is a wonderful gift.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: Natural abilities or talents.

Commentary: GIFT[+inherent]

Examples:
She has a gift for music.
He is fortunate to have so many gifts in the arts.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2

Sense Number 3: The act of giving.

Commentary: GIFT[+event]

Examples:
She made a large gift to the Red Cross.
I made a gift today to a local shelter.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 3
