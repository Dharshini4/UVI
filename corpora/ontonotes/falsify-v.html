falsify-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: make false by tampering with; forge

Examples:
Dr. Jacoby falsified the image by adding molecular weight markers.
The Commission's complaint alleges that Gordon falsified the
books and records.
United Front falsified the names of students who didn't really exist.
