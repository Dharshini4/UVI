training-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: acquiring skill

Commentary: TRAINING[+event] Refers to the act of practicing in order to gain skill.

Examples:
My dog's training program took three years.
John's martial arts training had made him a master ninja.
This person has undergone more extensive training than anyone I know.

Sense Number 2: result of being trained

Commentary: The attribute of a person who has been trained; the high level of skill a person possesses.

Examples:
She's a woman of excellent training and refinement.
Engineers from Intelli-Soft have the best training in the industry.
John used his martial arts training to win the tournament.
