restructure-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: construct or form anew

Examples:
After his accident, he had to restructure his life.
The team restructured the contracts of the quarterback and wide receiver.
In April 2005, the Army restructured the FCS program for two reasons.
While Copernicus restructured the view of the universe he did not remove Ptolemy's system of epicycles.
This has restructured the migration pattern that formerly New Zealand only accepted migrants from Europe.
