trash-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Dispose of something as useless and old; physically tear apart.

Examples:
Trash these old chairs.
He trashed her apartment and then called the cops to blame it on her.
Please trash the food scraps.

Sense Number 2: Express a totally negative opinion of.

Examples:
The critics really trashed the performance.
He trashed her in print and on television for months on end.
