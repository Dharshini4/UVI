post-v; 8 Senses

Sense Number 1: display or publish something publicly

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1[agent[human]] POST NP2 PP[locative]
NOTE: includes specific financial sense of making entries in ledgers, and transferring information from accounts receivable (AR) or accounts payable (AP) to ledgers.

Examples:
I'll post the news on the bulletin board.
Can you post a warning at the dump?
You need an account to post your resume on our site.
Please post comment on this blog.
The journal page reference and date entry was posted to the ledger.
Posting process has to be run in to post AR to Customer balance.
The bank posted the fee as a debit.
