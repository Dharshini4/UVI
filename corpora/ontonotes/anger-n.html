anger-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Belligerence aroused by a real or supposed wrong.

Commentary: ANGER[+event]

Examples:
I felt such anger towards him.
Anger is one of the deadly sins.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 3

Sense Number 2: The state of being angry.

Commentary: ANGERp[+state]

Examples:
She was in anger all day.
I fought with him in anger.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2
