resource-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: aid

Commentary: a source of aid or support that may be drawn upon when needed

Examples:
The local library is a valuable resource.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 1 2

Sense Number 2: resourcefulness

Commentary: imagination - the ability to deal resourcefully with unusual problems

Examples:
A man of resource.
The Professor demonstrated great resource by thinking of ways to get off of the island.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 3
