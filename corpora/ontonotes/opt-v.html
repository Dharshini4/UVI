opt-v; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: Select as an alternative over another.

Commentary: INCLUDES: OPT FOR, OPT TO

Examples:
She opted for the job on the East coast.
It remains to be seen how many managers will opt to retire.

Commentary: IS: OPT OUT

Examples:

We opted out of going to the restaurant.
