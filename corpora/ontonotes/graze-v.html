graze-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: (Cause to) feed, as if over a geographic area.

Commentary:
NOTE: May be metaphorical.

Examples:
The herd grazed in the meadow.
We grazed on a large buffet.
The rancher grazed the cattle for half a day.

Mappings:
VerbNet: dine-39.5
FrameNet: NM
PropBank: graze.01
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 3, 5

Sense Number 2: Break the skin (of a body part) by scraping; impact something else.

Commentary:
NOTE: While typically denoting injury to a body part, may apply to other circumstances.

Examples:
She was grazed by the stray bullet.
The instrument grazes the skin.
The second shot grazes her temple.

Mappings:
VerbNet: hurt-40.8.3,touch-20-1
FrameNet: Experience_bodily_harm,Impact,Cause_impact
PropBank: NM
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2, 4
