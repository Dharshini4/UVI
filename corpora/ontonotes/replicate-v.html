replicate-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Reproduce or make an exact copy of; make, do, or perform again.

Commentary: INCLUDES: REPLICATE BACK

Examples:
Scientists replicated the cell.
The human body replicates its own genetic information.
Authors don't usually replicate previous plotlines.
Can I replicate back to the server?
