regurgitate-v; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: (medicine) pour or rush back in.

Examples:
The blood regurgitates into the heart ventricle.
Fluid regurgitates into the stomach.

Sense Number 2: Vomit, either in a controlled or uncontrolled fashion.

Examples:
Birds often regurgitate food for their young.
She regurgitated all the beer she had drunk.
The patient regurgitated the contents of his stomach.

Sense Number 3: Repeat from memorization.
