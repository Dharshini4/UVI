quiver-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: An animate being shakes with fast, uncontrolled movements.

Commentary:
NOTE: shaking may be of a body part.

Examples:
His nostrils quivered at the smell of dinner.
He quivered with fear.
She quivers in his arms.

Mappings:
VerbNet: body_internal_states-40.6,modes_of_being_with_motion-47.3
FrameNet: NM
PropBank: quiver.01
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: An inanimate object moves back and forth quickly, in place.

Commentary:
NOTE: may be metaphorical.

Examples:
The candle quivered in the dark room.
The city quivered with excitement.

FrameNet: Moving_in_place
