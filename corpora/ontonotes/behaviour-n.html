behaviour-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: The action or reaction of something (as a machine or substance) under specified circumstances.

Commentary: BEHAVIOUR[+inanimate][+theme]

Examples:
The behaviour of small particles can be studied in experiments.
The behaviour of our program is unclear.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Commentary: BEHAVIOUR[+animate][+agent]

Examples:

Human behaviour is a fascinating object of study.
