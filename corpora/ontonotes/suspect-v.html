suspect-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: surmise, believe, consider or think something to be the case

Commentary: Syntax is: NP1 SUSPECT

Examples:
I suspect that we will be hearing more about this situation.
I suspect he is a fugitive.
She suspected that the neighbor's cat had chewed on her flowers.

Commentary: Syntax is: NP1 SUSPECT NP2

Examples:
