wobble-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Move unsteadily along a path.

Examples:
The drunk wobbled down the street.
The ship wobbled out of control.
I wobbled out of the office.

Sense Number 2: Tremble or shake in place.

Commentary:
NOTE: may be metaphorical.

Examples:
His voice wobbled with restrained emotion.
My knees wobbled and I sat down.
He wobbles back and forth as he speaks.
