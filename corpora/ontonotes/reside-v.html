reside-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: Live in a certain place; make one's home, be a native to a place, be inherent.

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1 RESIDE PP{P NP[location]}
NOTE: Second NP may be in NP2 position if a deictic pronoun, like 'here' or 'there.'

Examples:
My parents reside in Florida.
She resides in Princeton.
He resides in the two rooms on the top floor.
They all reside here, with me.
California resides on the West Coast.
Trees reside in the field.
Wild horses have resided here since time immemorial.
The existence of dark energy shows that space does not reside in the
lowest of these phases.
