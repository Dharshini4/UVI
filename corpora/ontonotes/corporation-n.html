corporation-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: business

Commentary: CORPORATION[+entity][+organization][+business][+legally_defined] a business firm whose articles of incorporation have been approved in some state

Examples:
I've invested in a large profitable corporation.
These corporations are publicly traded on the NYSE.
He started his own corporation a few years ago and now he is rich.
