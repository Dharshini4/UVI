delegation-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: representatives

Commentary: deputation, commission, delegacy, mission a group of representatives, or delegates

Examples:
The delegation decided to ban the sale of alcohol.
The delegation from Ireland was invited to join the President for tea.
Several members of the delegation decided to crash the party.
