unveil-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: remove cover to reveal something

Examples:
Olivia is eventually persuaded to unveil her face to Cesario.
He unveiled his true feelings as he expressed his disappointment and relief.
The memorial to those who had died in the war was unveiled in 1948.
A new government policy on forests is due to be unveiled in April.
