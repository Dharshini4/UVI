slow-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: lessen in intensity

Commentary:
NOTE: references to speed, productivity, magnitude, effect Includes: SLOW UP, SLOW DOWN

Examples:
Will you slow down for the sharp curves?
Not knowing what was around the corner, we slowed up.
Over the holiday period he slowed his blogging activities.
After installing the application, my system has slowed down.
Economic growth slowed by half.
HIV prevalence has slowed down.
As soon as the rain slowed, she walked back to her cabin.

Sense Number 2: hold back, retard, delay

Commentary: Delaying or becoming a stumbling block to progress.
NOTE: This sense has the inference that the cause of the slowing is unexpected and undesired, not a natural occurrence. The object being slowed is progressing less than desired and expected. Includes: SLOW UP, SLOW DOWN

Examples:
News of pandemonion may have slowed the much needed aid.
Science is being slowed down by fraud.
We got slowed up in Minburn by slow drivers.
