rewrite-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: Write differently or alter a piece or writing, perhaps to suit a different purpose.

Examples:
The student rewrote his thesis.
The group rewrites old plays for use in schools.
We rewrote the grant proposal for a new organization.
