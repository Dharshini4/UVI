injure-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: hurt or cause harm to

Examples:
She fell and injured her shoulder.
He claimed that working too hard was injuring his health.
She injured my feelings when she did not include me among her guests.
Such violations will seriously injure your reputation and that of your Company.
