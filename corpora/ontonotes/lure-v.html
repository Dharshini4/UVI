lure-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Provoke someone to do something through (often exaggerated or false) promises or persuasion.

Examples:
He lured me into buying the faulty television set.
She lured me into believing her false claims.

Mappings:

WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: Attract or draw near to oneself or a location.

Commentary:
NOTE: Location may be abstract.

Examples:
She lured me into temptation.
The predator lured the child into the forest.
