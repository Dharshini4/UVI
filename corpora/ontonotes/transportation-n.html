transportation-n; 5 Senses

Sense Number 1: transit

Commentary: transportation system - a facility consisting of the means and equipment necessary for the movement of passengers or goods

Examples:
My transportation is old and rusty.
Light rail transportation is clean and runs frequently between PA and NJ.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: moving

Commentary: TRANSPORTATION[+event] tranfer, transferral, conveyance - the act of transporting something form one location to another

Examples:
The transportation was much faster on SEPTA than in a city cab.
Transportation of perishables across country must be fast.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 2

Sense Number 3: freight rate

Commentary: the price paid for and the commercial enterprise of transporting goods and materials

Examples:
I hope that transportation of refrigerated items is charged by the pound.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 3 5

Sense Number 4: DoT

Commentary: Department of Transporation, Transportation, DoT - the United States federal department that institutes and coordinates national transportation programs; created in 1996

Examples:
The Department of Transporation is providing assistance to residents
affected by the local road blocks.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 4

Sense Number 5: exile

Commentary: TRANSPORTATION[+event] deportation, expatriation - The act of expelling a person from their native land

Examples:
Men in exile dream of hope.
His transportation to a penal colony was long.
The sentence was one of transportation for life.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 6
