problem-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: troubles

Commentary: job, trouble - a state or source of difficulty

Examples:
She and her husband are having problems.
It is always a job to contact him.
Urban problems such as traffic congestion and smog are reason enough to move.
One problem after another delayed the job.
What's the problem?

Examples:
