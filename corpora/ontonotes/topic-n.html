topic-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: subject

Commentary: Something that is talked about or thought about. Any subject matter.

Examples:
Let's talk about another topic for a while.
The topic of today's discussion is called, "Did Cavemen Kill the Dinosaurs?"
That's a very sensitive topic for me.
We're getting a bit off-topic today.
His letters are always about the same topic.
