director-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: decision maker for an organization

Commentary: DIRECTOR of NP[+organization]

Examples:
He is now serving as managing director of flight operations.
Mary was the director of the Center for Disease Control.
The new director of the ballet company is an effective fundraiser.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 2, 3

Sense Number 2: person providing artistic guidance to specific artistic endeavors

Commentary: DIRECTOR of NP[+countable][+artistic]

Examples:
Who was the director of those three films?
John has been asked to be the director of this year's Messiah Sing.
She was fired for not heeding the director's stage directions.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 4
