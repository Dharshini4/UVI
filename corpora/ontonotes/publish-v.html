publish-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: bring to public attention through production, distribution or sale

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1[agent] PUBLISH NP2
NOTE: Usually involves printed material.

Examples:
That press published 10,000 copies of her first book of poetry.
Our local magazine is publishing that story in next month's issue.
Noone would publish his novel during his lifetime.
That author has published twelve volumes of children's stories.
She is publishing her first collection of poetry.
The names of the winners of the competition will be published in June.
She was only 19 when her first novel was published.
We publish for local authors.
