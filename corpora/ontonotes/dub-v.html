dub-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: give a name or title to

Examples:
He was dubbed The Hall Monitor because of his respect for rules.
TV Guide dubbed him one of TV's Ten Most Powerful Stars.
Before his death, the British crown dubbed him Sir William.

Sense Number 2: provide with a soundtrack or voiceover

Examples:
The two leads had their singing voices dubbed.
They changed the name, dubbed the film and marketed it as a Hong Kong comedy.
It has a score that is so out of place it sounds dubbed.
