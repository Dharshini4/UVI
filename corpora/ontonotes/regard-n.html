regard-n; 6 Senses

Sense Number 1: aspect

Commentary: respect - usually preceded by 'in'; a detail point

Examples:
It differs in that regard.
George and Fido like peanut butter, they're the same in that regard.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: heed

Commentary: attentiveness, paying attention - paying particular notice (as to children or helpless people)

Examples:
His regard to her wishes pleased her.
He spends without regard for the consequences.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 2

Sense Number 3: compliments

Commentary: wish - (usually plural) a polite expression of desire for someone's welfare

Examples:
Give him my kind regards.
My best regards.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 3

Sense Number 4: esteem

Commentary: respect - the condition of being honored (esteemed or respected or well regarded); an attitude of admiration or esteem

Examples:
It is held in high regard.
A man who has earned high regard.
She lost all regard for him.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 5 7

Sense Number 5: respect

Commentary: a feeling of friendship and esteem

Examples:
She mistook his manly regard for love.
He inspires regard.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 6

Sense Number 6: gaze

Commentary: a long fixed look

Examples:
He fixed his paternal regard on me.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 4
