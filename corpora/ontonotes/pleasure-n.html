pleasure-n; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: A feeling of joy or contentment.

Commentary: PLEASURE[+emotion]
NOTE: usually expressed as "with pleasure"

Examples:
He was tingling with pleasure.
I was filled with pleasure.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: Something that affords enjoyment.

Commentary: PLEASURE[+object]

Examples:
It was a true pleasure to behold.
He puts duty before pleasure.
The new car is a pleasure to drive.
He took his pleasure of her.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2, 4, 5

Sense Number 3: A formal expression concerning duty.

Commentary: PLEASURE[+obligation]

Examples:
He serves at the pleasure of the President.
I serve at the pleasure of the Queen.
We all work at the pleasure of our CEO.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 3
