store-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: put away or hold for storage or future use

Commentary: Includes: STORE UP. Syntax is: NP1 STORE NP2

Examples:
Can you store port the same way as wine?
There's this buried palace that once stored the literary treasures.
All my records are stored in this cabinet.
