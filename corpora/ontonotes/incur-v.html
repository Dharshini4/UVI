incur-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: to undergo an unpleasant experience or burden

Examples:
People who smoke incur a great danger to their health.
Who pays if I incur an injury due to an auto accident or my car is damaged?
Seniors incur 42% of all US drug costs.
Is the landlord liable for the damages incurred by a tenant?
