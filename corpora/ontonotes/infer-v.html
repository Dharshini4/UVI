infer-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: Conclude by reasoning, guess correctly, or come to believe to be true.

Examples:
He inferred the number of beans in the jar by comparing to other
known quantities.
May I infer that you're here for the job?
Scientists can only infer the age of the universe.
We can infer a lot from facial gestures.
