disengage-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: become free of or released from

Examples:
It might be best to disengage the autopilot and fly manually.
Meanwhile McCubby had disengaged himself from the melee.
The bolts disengaged.
It will be difficult for you to disengage unless you are challanged.
The US can't afford to completely disengage from the Middle East.
Rejected children also have a tendency to disengage social environments.
