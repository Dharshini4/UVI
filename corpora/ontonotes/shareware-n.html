shareware-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: evaluation computer programs distributed freely with optional fees charged for further use or additional features.

Commentary: SHAREWARE[+information][+program][+evaluation][+computer][+distributed][+free_of_charge][+with_optional][+fees]

Examples:
John didn't think the shareware application he downloaded worked well.
