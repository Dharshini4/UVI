rationalize-v; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: cause to seem reasonable or rational

Examples:
He rationalized involving his brother, son and friend in espionage.
He rationalized, for his peace of mind, that by shooting the two Viet Cong he had
derailed a whole chain of events.
She rationalizes that because he looks good in a tuxedo, he must be on her side.
The teacher is creative and adaptive, she shares responsibilities for the environment
with students, and she rationalizes rules in group sessions.
We could try to rationalize these sitings away, but I think there's something to them.

certainly the most important step to take to rationalize our food system.
