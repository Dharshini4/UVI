Examples:

It was smart enough to elude capture.
But successful, moneymaking blogs elude most people.

I had the sense that this missing element would forever elude me.
And still some of those females manage to elude their bodygaurds.
Peace and stability will continue to elude it.
Elude the cops first then settle who gets the case.
