scruple-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: hesitate on moral ground

Examples:
Don't scruple too much before you pick it up.
He doesn't scruple to correct me when he thinks I'm wrong.
Her man does not scruple to acquire her hand by military force.
Don't scruple about my feelings!
They will never scruple to sell the use of their bodies.
I do not scruple to follow Richter.
He doesn't scruple to do it in front of Audrey herself.
