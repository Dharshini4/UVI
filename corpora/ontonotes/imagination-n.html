imagination-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: The ability (individual or collective) to produce images or scenarios not currently in reality.

Commentary: IMAGINATION[+mental]

Examples:
Popular imagination created a world of demons.
Imagination reveals what the world could be.
He could still hear her in his imagination.
She is a woman of imagination.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 2, 3
