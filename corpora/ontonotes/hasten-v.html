hasten-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: speed up the process of; move at high speed

Examples:

Have we hastened the demise of the universe just by looking at it?
Riots hastened the decline of the once-vibrant corridor.
Fred hastened down the street because he was late, once again.
