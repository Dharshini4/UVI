twine-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: Spin, wind, or twist together, perhaps producing something.

Examples:
Twine the threads into a rope.
Twine the rope around itself.
Twine the rope around the pole.
She twined her arms around the child.
She twines her hair around her finger.
