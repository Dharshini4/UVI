riffle-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Look through a book or some other written material (for information).

Commentary: Is: RIFFLE THROUGH

Examples:
He riffled through the report.
She riffles through the text.
He riffles through poetry, not understanding a word.

Mappings:
VerbNet: investigate-35.4
FrameNet: NP
PropBank: riffle.01
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2

Sense Number 2: (Cause to) flutter about or be mixed.

Examples:
The paper riffled in the wind.
Water riffled during the earthquake.
Riffle the deck and then deal the cards.
