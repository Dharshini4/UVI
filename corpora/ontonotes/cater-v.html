cater-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: CATER TO: Give what is desired or needed.

Commentary: Is: CATER TO

Examples:
The company caters to the needs of young professionals.
The loudest of these reformers are money managers who cater to
smaller investors.
She caters to his every desire.
