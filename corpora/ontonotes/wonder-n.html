wonder-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: marvel

Commentary: something that causes feelings of wonder

Examples:
The wonders of modern science are responsible for our comfortable lifestyles.
The pyramids are one of the seven wonders of the ancient world.
That thing in your sink is a squishy wonder.

Mappings:
PropBank: wonder.01
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 2

Sense Number 2: curiosity

Commentary: WONDER[+event] wonderment, admiration - a state in which you want to learn more about something

Examples:
The child's wonder would only occupy him for a short time.
The green squishy thing in your sink creates wonder.
