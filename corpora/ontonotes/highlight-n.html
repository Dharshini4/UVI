highlight-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: the most memorable segments of an event

Commentary: HIGHLIGHT[+event][+segment][+memorable]

Examples:
The highlight of the night was when John got up and sang.
The evening news showed highlights from today's game.
The pitcher said last season was the highlight of his career so far.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: the quality of heightened light on areas of an object

Commentary: HIGHLIGHT[+quality][+visual][+of_light][+heightened]

Examples:
The highlights on the faces in the painting were done in pale blues.
Sally added some golden highlights to her hair with a rinse.
