swap-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: exchange or give something in exchange for

Commentary:
NOTE: Includes metaphorical usage.
NOTE: Includes SWAP OUT.

Examples:
He swapped the bottle for a Bible.
Why I swapped The Clash for Chopin.
You can swap the left and right mouse buttons.
You can swap Coke Zero with Coke and never know the difference!
My grandfather and his friends like to swap war stories.
We swapped out the old fans for new ones.
