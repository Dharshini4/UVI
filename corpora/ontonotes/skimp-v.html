skimp-v; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: Work (with) hastily or carelessly.

Commentary: INCLUDES: SKIMP OVER

Examples:
The only area where I felt he skimped over was Chinese civilisation.
I also disagree with how he skimped over the combat.

Mappings:
VerbNet: NP
FrameNet: NP
PropBank: NM
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1
WordNet Verb Particle Constructions, Multiword Expressions:
skimp_over 1

Sense Number 2: Limit in quantity or quality.

Commentary:
NOTE: This sense requires a recipient of the thing not being given, whereas Sense 3 requires the giving to be to oneself.

Examples:
We skimped on our giving that year.
She really skimps when tipping.

Mappings:
VerbNet: NP
FrameNet: NP
PropBank: skimp.01
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2, 4

Sense Number 3: Subsist on a meager allowance or supply.

Examples:
We skimped and scratched to make a living.
The family skimped in order to avoid starvation.

VerbNet: NP
FrameNet: NP
PropBank: NM
