merger-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: method of consolidating separate things

Commentary: MERGER[+action][+method][+consolidation]

Examples:
The proposed merger of the two largest banks is under governmental review.
John and Mary like to refer to their marriage as a friendly merger.
The judge may incorporate the two misdemeanor charges into a merger with the felony charge.
The merger agreement is more than two hundred pages in length.
