yell-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: utter a loud cry or communicate in such a voice

Examples:
He yelled when I stepped on his foot.
I can hear you just fine--you don't have to yell.
"Dinner's ready," she yelled.
The monkeys yelled to each other through the trees.
