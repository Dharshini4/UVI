Sense Number 2: pay a tithe, usually one tenth of one's income

Examples:
Churches ask that people who tithe give nearly five times that.
Local broadcasters should tithe the income that they get from these ads.
Quentin Richardson should tithe to Steve Nash.
They tithe 10 percent to St. Richard's.
If you don't have income, you don't tithe.
Quite concretely, Jacob promises to tithe
