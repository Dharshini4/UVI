hide-v; 1 Senses

Probably his horse would be close to where he was hiding.
She is hiding out in a cabin in Montana.
Don't trust a supplier that tries to hide the facts!
She tried to hide her disappointment at not getting the promotion.
This ancient historic temple is hidden in mystery.
I feel sure there's something about her past that she's trying to hide from me.
