potter-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: Move or work (as if) aimlessly or casually.

Commentary: Includes: POTTER AROUND

Examples:
The woman potters around in her yard each day.
We pottered around the school.
Don't potter!
She pottered in the lobby for hours.
