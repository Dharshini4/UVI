squint-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: cross or partly close your eyes (to look at)

Examples:
I squinted my eyes as the sun hit my windshield.
Jerry squinted at the dog, trying to figure out what breed it was.
Grandpa used to always squint at the fine print of the newspaper.
