contract-v; 5 Senses

Sense Number 1: undertake, engage in

Commentary: Includes: CONTRACT OUT

Examples:
We contracted to have them paint just the exterior of the building.
They have contracted a new pitcher for next season.
The firm has contracted out the Bernulli project.

Sense Number 5: consent/refuse to pay money to a trade union for political use

Commentary: Includes: CONTRACT IN, CONTRACT OUT
NOTE: 'contract in' is 'consent to pay' and 'contract out' is 'refuse to pay'.

Examples:
If you contract in, the additional state pension assumes everyone is married.
If you contract out via a personal pension, you will enjoy a slightly higher rebate.
