rustle-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: (Cause to) make a dry crackling sound, possibly by mixing.

Examples:
The leaves rustled in the Fall wind.
I rustled the papers over the desk.
Try not to rustle the forms.

Mappings:
VerbNet: sound_emission-43.2
FrameNet: Motion_noise,Make_noise,Cause_impact
PropBank: rustle.01
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: Forage or take, perhaps illegally.

Examples:
In the olden days, outlaws would rustle cattle.
The pig rustled for acorns.
