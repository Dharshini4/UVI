signify-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: stand for, indicate

Commentary: Syntax is: NP1[agent] SIGNIFY NP2 NP1[instrument] SIGNIFY NP2[patient]

Examples:
This morpheme signifies the plural in Spanish.
'It is the tale told by an idiot, full of sound and fury, signifying nothing.' -Hamlet
The driver signified his intention to make a left turn.
