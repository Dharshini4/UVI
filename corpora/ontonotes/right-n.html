right-n; 7 Senses

Sense Number 1: liberties

Commentary: an abstract idea of that which is due a person or governmental body by law or tradition or nature (typically legal rights)

Examples:
They are endowed by thier Creator with certain unalienable Rights.
"Certain rights can never granted to the government but must be kept in the hands of the people." -Eleanor Roosevelt
A right is not something that somebody give you it is something that nobody can take away.
The author of my favorite book sold the film rights recently.

Sense Number 2: a position beside

Commentary: location near or direction toward the right side; the side to the south when a person faces east

Examples:
He stood on the right.
Follow the road for 2.3 miles and my house will be on the right.

Sense Number 3: a turn

Commentary: a turn to the right

Examples:

Sense Number 4: right wing

Commentary: those who support political or social or economic conservatism, those who believe that things are better left unchanged

Examples:
