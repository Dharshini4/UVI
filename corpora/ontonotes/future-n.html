future-n; 4 Senses

Sense Number 1: time to come

Commentary: hereafter, futurity - the time yet to come

Examples:
In the future we will drive flying cars.
What will you do in the future.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: verb tense

Commentary: a verb tense that expresses actions or states in the future

Examples:
The paper was written in future tense.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 2

Sense Number 3: commodities

Commentary: bulk commodities bought or sold at an agreed price for delivery at a specified future date

Examples:
He made his living trading in futures.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 3

Sense Number 4: personal time to come

Commentary: a time yet to come for a specific person (usually possessive)

Examples:
My future is bright.
What will you do with your future?

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: -
