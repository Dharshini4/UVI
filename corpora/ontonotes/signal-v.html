signal-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: communicate or make known by gestures, action, or signs

Commentary: Syntax is: NP1 SIGNAL (NP2)

Examples:
She signaled her impatience.
They have signaled their willingness to negotiate.
I signaled the driver to proceed.
Will you signal now?

Commentary: Syntax is: NP1 SIGNAL NP2

Examples:
