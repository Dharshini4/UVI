disapprove-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: consider bad, wrong, or inappropriate

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1[experiencer] DISAPPROVE (PP{of NP})

Examples:
Her mother disapproved of her new job.
I disapprove of her child-rearing methods.
A majority of the respondents said they disapproved of the performance of the Democratic-lead Congress.
You are seeing a married man? I disapprove.

Sense Number 2: refuse to approve

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1[agent] DISAPPROVE NP2

Examples:
They disapproved my extension. What do I do now?
The shareholders disapproved the acquisition of the company by Acme Inc. for $27 a share.
