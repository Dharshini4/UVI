intimidate-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: Make timid or fearful, possibly either coercing or preventing some activity.

Examples:
Her boss intimidates her.
Abuse victims are all too often intimidated into silence by their
abusers.
You don't intimidate me, missy!
The lawyer intimidated the witness into testifying.
