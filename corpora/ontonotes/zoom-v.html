zoom-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: move (usu. in a positive direction) quickly

Commentary: Includes: ZOOM ALONG

Examples:
The cars zoomed down the highway.
We were zooming along on this assignment, when the power went out.
The subway zoomed past us as we stood on the deck.
