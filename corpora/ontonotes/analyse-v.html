analyse-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: investigate or study methodically in great detail

Examples:
We will analyse two Shakespearian sonnets tomorrow.
Researchers analysed the purchases of 6300 households.
Water samples taken from streams were analysed for contamination by chemicals.
My job is to analyse today's financial market.
