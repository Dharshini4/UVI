cloister-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Surround a garden.

Commentary:
NOTE: Thing surrounded must be a garden.

Examples:
We should cloister the garden.
Monks cloistered the gardens.

Mappings:
VerbNet: concealment-16,pocket-9.10
FrameNet: NP
PropBank: NM
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 2

Sense Number 2: Seclude from the world.

Commentary:
NOTE: Thing secluded is often a person.

Examples:
She cloistered herself in the office.
He cloisters his daughter from unworthy men.
He cloistered himself from all experience.
