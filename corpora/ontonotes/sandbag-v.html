sandbag-v; 4 Senses

Sense Number 1: Abuse, (as if) by hitting.

Commentary:
NOTE: May be metaphorical and/or reflexive.

Examples:
They sandbagged the politician.
Don't sandbag me!
He sandbagged himself into a corner.
The team sandbagged themselves for the last game.

Mappings:
VerbNet: NM
FrameNet: NP
PropBank: NM
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 3

Sense Number 2: Compel by coercion, threats, or other means.

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1[agent] SANDBAG NP2[patient] to COMP

Examples:
They sandbagged him to make dinner for everyone.

Mappings:
VerbNet: force-59
FrameNet: NP
PropBank: NM
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2

Sense Number 3: Downplay one's ability in order to deceive.

Examples:

Examples:
