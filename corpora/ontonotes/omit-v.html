omit-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: leave out, exclude

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1[agent] OMIT NP2[patient] (PP[locative])

Examples:
She told me the most unfortunate vicissitudes of her life, not omitting a single detail.
I have omitted some variables altogether from the original.
The final version of the memorandum omitted the language regarding extreme pressure.

Mappings:
VerbNet: remove-10.1
FrameNet: NP
PropBank: omit.01
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: neglect, fail to do

Commentary: Syntax Includes: NP1[agent] OMIT [predicate] NP1[agent] OMIT NP2[patient]

Examples:
The article omitted to mention victims of left wing revolutionary politics.
They specifically omitted to protect the privacy of the commercial airline that assisted in this study.
Don't omit to have your address either written or printed on the corner of every envelope.
You omitted the duties of the package owner.
Remember the need to take the medication as it is ordered, not omitting a single dose. (to take a single dose)
