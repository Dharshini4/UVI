interoperability-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: the ability of computers to exchange digital information between them and make use of it

Commentary: INTEROPERABILITY[+quality][+capability][+between_computers][+for_exchange][+information][+digital]

Examples:
These computer programs have very limited interoperability.
The common language enabled large-scale interoperability across whole networks of heterogeneous computers.
