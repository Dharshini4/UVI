gurgle-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Move along a path while producing a bubbling noise, as of liquids.

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1[theme] GURGLE PP[directional]

Examples:
The stream gurgled from town to town.
Water gurgled from the bottle as he drank.
Fluid gurgles from my engine block whenever I start the car.

Mappings:
VerbNet: sound_emission-43.2
FrameNet: Motion_noise,Make_noise
PropBank: NP
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 3

Sense Number 2: Produce sounds or utterances reminiscent of a bubbling noise of liquids.

Commentary:
NOTE: In Sense 2, the noise is not a result of motion along a path.

Examples:
The baby gurgled with satisfaction when the mother tickled it.
The stabbing victim gurgled for help.
Stop gurgling and answer the question!
The machine finally gurgled on.

Mappings:
VerbNet: sound_emission-43.2,manner_speaking-37.3
FrameNet: Make_noise,Motion_noise,Communication_noise
PropBank: NP
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2, 4
