recite-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Narrate or perform, possibly from memory.

Examples:
She recited a poem.
The pupil recited his lesson for the day.
They recited the pardon written for the occasion.
The father recited the story to his child.
The police asked the witnesses to recite the occurances of the
previous evening.

Sense Number 2: Specify in list form, not as a narrative.

Examples:
She recited the many criminal offenses she had committed on her job application.
The doctor recited the list of possible side effects of the drug.
