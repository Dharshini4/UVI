creation-n; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: The human act of making something new.

Commentary: CREATION[+event][+human_agent]

Examples:
The creation of the stadium was a major achievement.
Creation is a wonderful process for anyone.
From its creation the plan was doomed.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 3, 4

Sense Number 2: The object brought into existence.

Commentary: CREATION[+theme]

Examples:
She made of herself a new creation.
After he made the sculpture, his creation sat untouched.
The biggest trees in all creation are in that forest.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2, 6

Sense Number 3: (Theology) God's act of bringing the universe into existence.

Commentary: CREATION[+theology]

Examples:
The Creation was an act of awesome power.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 5
