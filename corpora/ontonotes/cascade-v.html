cascade-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: rush down in big quantities

Commentary: Can be used metaphorically.

Examples:
Her black hair cascaded down her back.
The rainwater cascaded down our shingle 'beach' area and flowed
over the top of the retaining wall.

Sense Number 2: arrange (open windows) on a computer desktop so that they overlap each other

Examples:
The Cascade Windows button cascades windows.
The script actually cascades the windows.
