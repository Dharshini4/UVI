blaze-v; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: Shine brightly and intensely, possibly along a trajectory or as a result of burning.

Examples:
Meteors blazed across the atmosphere.
The summer sun alone can cause a pine to blaze.
The spaceship blazed out into space.
Garish neon marquees blaze along the main street.
