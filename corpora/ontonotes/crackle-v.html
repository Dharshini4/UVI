crackle-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Emit a popping or crunching sound.

Examples:
Rice Krispies snap, crackle, and pop.
His shoes crackled on the gravel.
Wood crackled in the fireplace.

Sense Number 2: (Cause to) cover with small cracks or fissures.

Examples:
The blazing sun crackled the desert sand.
Wind and rain crackled the paint.
