exempt-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: free from a requirement or liability

Examples:
He exempted the institution on the basis of the lack of precedent in the case.
The law exempts food from sales tax.
He exempted hotels from the evacuation order.
A score of 1070 or higher will exempt students from TSI testing in reading and writing.
