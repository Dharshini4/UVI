quarter-n; 12 Senses

Sense Number 1: U.S. coin

Commentary: a United States coin worth one fourth of a dollar

Examples:
He fed four quarters into the slot machine.
I found a quarter in the street today a treated myself to some gum.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 10

Sense Number 2: one-fourth

Commentary: fourth, fourth part, twenty-five percent, quartern - one of four equal parts

Examples:
A quarter of pound of beef cost me five dollars at that store.
The robbers split the loot into four and each took a quarter.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 3: sports game

Commentary: QUARTER[+event] one of four period of play into which some games are divided

Examples:
There were so many time-outs that the first quarter lasted two hours.
Both teams scored in the first quarter.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 3

Sense Number 4: city district

Commentary: a district of a city having some distinguishing character

Examples:
Our hotel was in the Latin Quarter.
The casbah is an older or native quarter of many cities in northern Africa.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 2

Sense Number 5: 15 minutes

Commentary: a unit of time equal to 15 minutes or a quarter of an hour

Examples:
It's a quarter til 4.
He didn't tell me until a quarter after four o'clock.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 4

Sense Number 6: school marking period

Commentary: QUARTER[+event] one of four periods into which the school year is divided

Examples:
The last quarter of my academic career will end in June.
The fall quarter ends at Christmas.
She was so sick that she missed the whole quarter.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 5

Sense Number 7: one fourth of a year

Commentary: QUARTER[+event] a fourth part of a year; three months

Examples:
Unemployment fell during the last quarter.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 6

Sense Number 8: compass direction

Commentary: one of the four major divisions of the compass

Examples:
The wind is coming from that quarter.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 7

Sense Number 9: unspecified person

Commentary: an unspecified person

Examples:
He dropped a word in the right quarter.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 11

Sense Number 10: ship tail

Commentary: stern, after part, poop - the rear part of a ship

Examples:
He was caught sleeping in the ship's quarter deck.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 12

Sense Number 11: shoe heel

Commentary: piece of leather that comprises the part of a shoe or boot covering the heel and joining the vamp

Examples:
George borrowed my nail gun and now his shoe has one hole in the quarter.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 13

Commentary: a quarter of a hundredweight

Examples:
