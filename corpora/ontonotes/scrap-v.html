scrap-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: dispose of or break up into parts

Examples:
Microsoft has scrapped the defragmenter display in Vista and
automated the process.
I'm not totally surprised that they scrapped the dual HDMI.
The White House scrapped the idea of putting the occupation force
under UN mandate.
The person who bought the car from us wrecked it, then scrapped it for parts.

WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 3

Sense Number 2: have a disagreement or quarrel over something

Commentary: Includes SCRAP OVER.

Examples:
Our leaders are literally scrapping over bones.
Its opponents are scrapping over which currency can sink the
lowest, the fastest.
