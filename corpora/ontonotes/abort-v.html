abort-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Terminate before completion.

Examples:
We have to abort the mission.
"Abort!" the captain yelled.
She helped me to abort the process running on my computer.

Sense Number 2: (Cause to) stop developing and die, as a fetus during pregnancy.

Examples:
The fetus spontaneously aborted.
We chose to abort during our first pregnancy.
Some would take away a woman's right to abort her fetus.
