phrase-n; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: An expression consisting of one or more words.

Commentary: PHRASE[+linguistic]

Examples:
He uttered several phrases.
That phrase doesn't fit with the rest of the poem.
It is a common phrase in English.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 3

Sense Number 2: A short musical passage.

Commentary: PHRASE[+music]

Examples:
Play through the end of the phrase.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2

Sense Number 3: Dance movements that are linked in a single choreographic sequence.

Commentary: PHRASE[+dance]

Examples:

Mappings:
