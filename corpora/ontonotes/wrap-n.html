wrap-n; 5 Senses

Sense Number 1: an outer, enclosing garment

Commentary: WRAP[+artifact][+garment][+enclosing][+outer]

Examples:
Mary wore a chiffon wrap over her evening dress.
The children left their wraps in the cloak room.
The skirt is an ankle-length wrap of dark gray merino wool.

Sense Number 2: a rolled up sandwich

Commentary: WRAP[+artifact][+food][+sandwich][+rolled_up]

Examples:

We enjoyed the Vietnamese lettuce wraps filled with spicy meat.

PropBank: NM

Commentary: WRAP[+artifact][+cover][+enveloping][+of_material][+flat]
