subscribe-v; 4 Senses

Sense Number 1: pay an amount of money or receive something regularly

Commentary: Includes: SUBSCRIBE TO

Examples:
Are you subscribed to the About Web Design newsletter?
More than 200,000 viewers subscribed to the site.
I haven't subscribed to the Economist in years.
Have you ever subscribed to a Ladies' Charity in London?
