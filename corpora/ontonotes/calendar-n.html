calendar-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: A system of timekeeping that defines the beginning, length, and divisions of the year.

Commentary: CALENDAR[+abstract]

Examples:
The Mayan calendar is still one of the most advanced systems.
The Gregorian calendar defines a certain number of months.

Mappings:

Commentary: CALENDAR[+concrete]
