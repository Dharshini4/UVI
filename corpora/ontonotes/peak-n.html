peak-n; 4 Senses

Sense Number 1: mountaintop

Commentary: peak, crown, crest, top, tip, summit - the top point of something

Examples:
The view from the peak was magnificent.
They clambered to the summit of Monanock.
At the peak of the pyramid.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 4 6

Examples:
voltage peak...
The peak of his career was in the 1960s.
His landscapes were deemed the peak of beauty.
The artist's gifts are at their peak.
At the peak of her career she was mediocre at best.
The peak of perfection...

The peak of his ambition lasted through his retirement.
So many peaks achieved by man...
At the peak of his performance he was the best.

Examples:
