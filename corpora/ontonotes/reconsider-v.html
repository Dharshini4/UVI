reconsider-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: consider again, usually with a view to changing

Examples:
At the last minute she reconsidered and did not bring the charges.
I suppose either she reconsidered or a superior told her to unlock it.
Larry Craig is reconsidering his decision to resign.
Our Legislature is reconsidering Senate Bill 840, which was vetoed by the governor last year.
