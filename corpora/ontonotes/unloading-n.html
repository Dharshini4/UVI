unloading-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: act of removing

Commentary: UNLOADING[+event] the labor of unloading something

Examples:
The unloading of the cargo takes two hours.
The unloading was very difficult.
The unloading of your couches off of the truck hurt my back.
