value-n; 6 Senses

Sense Number 1: measure of usefulness, desirability

Commentary: VALUE[+quality][+usefulness][+desirability] The quality that renders something desirable or useful or important

Examples:
His knowledge of systems engineering will be of great value to this project.
George makes rude comments for shock value.
There is no value in getting to the airport three hours early.
These shoppers are looking for value but don't want to pay premium prices.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2

Sense Number 2: price or financial worth of something

Commentary: VALUE[+quantity][+money][+price][+market]

Examples:
Homes have lost 10 percent of their value in the past year.
The land increased in value as the urban center expanded.
What is the value of their company's stock today?

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 3

Sense Number 3: moral principal

Commentary: VALUE[+information][+principles][+social][+ethical] An ideal accepted by some individual or group

Examples:
He has old fashioned values.
The candidates all claim to endorse family values.
Personal liberty is a fundamental value of democracies.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 6

Sense Number 4: duration of a musical note

Commentary: VALUE[+quantity][+duration][+auditory][+note][+musical] The relative duration of a musical note, time value, note value

Examples:
A dotted eighth note has a greater time value than a simple eighth note.
Triplets take up the same space as two notes of that value.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 5

Sense Number 5: relative darkness of a color

Commentary: VALUE[+quantity][+measure][+of_darkness][+color] Relative darkness or lightness of a color

Examples:
I establish the colors and principal values by organizing the painting
into three values - dark, medium... and light"- Joe Hing Lowe

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 4

Sense Number 6: mathematical magnitude, computed result

Commentary: VALUE[+quantity][+magnitude][+calculated]

Examples:
The physicist calculated an accurate value for the mass of Jupiter.
What is the mean value of x for this set of data points?
