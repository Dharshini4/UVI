slog-v; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: Work persistently.

Commentary: Is: SLOG AWAY

Examples:
She is slogging away at her dissertation.
He slogs away at a better fast ball.
They slogged away at the program all afternoon.

Sense Number 2: Walk heavily or firmly.

Examples:

Mules slogged in a tight circle.

Sense Number 3: Strike heavily, especially with a fist or implement.

Examples:
He slogged her right in the face.
I slogged the dog to get it off of me.
He slogged me so hard I passed out.
