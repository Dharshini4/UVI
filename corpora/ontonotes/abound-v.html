abound-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: be abundant or abundantly supplied with

Examples:
Surfing sun and activities abound at the Durban Beach fest.
The holiday spirit abounded in the barracks just before cadets left
Tuesday for Christmas furlough.
Scientists say dairy farming abounded in ancient Britain.
The waters abounded with flying-fish.
These woods abound in wild mushrooms.
