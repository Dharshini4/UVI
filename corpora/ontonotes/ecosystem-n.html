ecosystem-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: a community of interacting living organisms of diverse species, living in a habitat together

Commentary: ECOSYSTEM[+community][+organism][+living][+interacting][+in_habitat]

Examples:
A coral reef is a complex ecosystem of fish, algae and corals, as well as other small creatures.
Pollution and drought have both stressed the forest's ecosystem.
