transvestite-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: a person, usually a male, who dresses and behaves like a member of the opposite sex, i.e., usually as a woman

Commentary: TRANSVESTITE[+person][+with_appearance][+with_mannerisms][+of_opposite_sex]

Examples:
The tall well-dressed women in the bar were actually transvestites.
John decided to go to the Halloween party as a transvestite.
