letter-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: earn a letter, as as in school sports or clubs

Commentary: Syntax includes: NP1[agent[animate]] LETTER PP[theme]

Examples:
He attended New Bedford HS where he lettered in football and track and field.
He lettered in golf, band and debate/forensics and was named to the honor roll.
She lettered in cross country and basketball as well at Piper.
He lettered in baseball four years and lettered in tennis two years.
Doug Smith Lettered in band.

Sense Number 2: mark with lettering

Commentary: Syntax includes: NP1[agent[animate]] LETTER NP2[destination] (PP[theme]) (PP[instrument])

Examples:
Currently, he is lettering various comics.
Eight wooden blocks are lettered, and are placed in a box.
They can be custom lettered with up to 4 lines of text.
The end of each trunk was lettered: "A. Jones Well".
The front cover was lettered with a brush and a 10 year old bottle of india ink.
