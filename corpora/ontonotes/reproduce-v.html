reproduce-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: procreate, multiply

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1[agent] REPRODUCE

Examples:
Rabbits reproduced faster that farmers could kill them with
pitchforks.
When the animal reproduced, the DNA would be transmitted to some
fraction of its progeny.

Sense Number 2: make a copy, recreate, repeat

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1[agent] REPRODUCE NP2

Examples:
He reproduced the works of the Dutch masters, in particular the
portraits of Rubens, Hals, Van Dyke, and his master Soutman.
On May 30, he reproduced and posted on his blog a Xinhua news
story.
He reproduced the antique vestiges of powerful Rome and was one of
the most authoritative archeologists of his time.
