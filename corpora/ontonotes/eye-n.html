eye-n; 4 Senses

Sense Number 1: The organ of sight.

Commentary: EYE[+body_part]

Examples:
Her eyes were blue.
We removed the eye for the fetal pig dissection.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: (Good) discernment (either visiually or as if visually)

Commentary: EYE[+judgement]

Examples:
He tried to catch her eye.
She has an eye for fresh talent.
He has an artist's eye.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2, 3

Sense Number 3: An area that is central within some larger region.

Commentary: EYE[+center]

Examples:
They were in the eye of the storm.
We ran into the eye of the struggle.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 4

Sense Number 4: A small hole or loop (as in a needle).

Commentary: EYE[+concrete]

Examples:
The thread wouldn't go through the eye of the needle.
Needle threaders have large eyes.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 5
