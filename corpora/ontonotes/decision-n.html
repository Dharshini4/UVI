decision-n; 5 Senses

Sense Number 1: a position

Commentary: conclusion, determination - a position or opinion or judgement reached after consideration

Examples:
A decision unfavorable to the opposition.
His decision took the evidence into account.
Satisfied with the panel's decision...
Make a decision.
That was a good decision.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: act of deciding

Commentary: DECISION[+event] conclusion, determination - the act of making up your mind about something

Examples:
Decision on the policy took the panel almost all day.
The burden of decision was his.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 2

Sense Number 3: decisiveness

Commentary: the trait of resoluteness as evidenced by firmness of character or purpose

Examples:
A man of unusual decision.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 5

Sense Number 4: game outcome

Commentary: the outcome of a game or contest

Examples:
The team dropped three decisions in a row.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 4

Sense Number 5: victory

Commentary: (boxing) a victory won on points when no knockout has occurred

Examples:
He had little trouble in taking a unanimous decision over his opponent.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 3
