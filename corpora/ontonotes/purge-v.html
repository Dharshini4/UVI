purge-v; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: make clean, remove impurities

Examples:
The baby was born drunk and his tiny body shook with tremors as the alcohol was purged from his system.
I've purged the demons from my evil, twisted skeptical mind.
Purge the old gas tank.
The human life reached spiritual development, completely purged of all the delusion.
Aloes may have purged the poison.
