moor-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: (Cause to) land in or as if in a berth or dock, secured as with ropes.

Commentary: Includes: MOOR UP

Examples:
The ship moored for the evening.
The captain decided to moor at the island.
Moor up the boat.
