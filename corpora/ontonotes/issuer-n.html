issuer-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: an official organization that distributes authorized items, e.g., securities, publications, currency

Commentary: ISSUER[+organization][+institution][+distributor][+official][+of_items][+authorized]

Examples:
NOAA is the issuer of the most authoritative nautical charts.
The issuer of this rare coin is engraved on the back.
