edge-v; 6 Senses

Sense Number 1: advance or cause to advance slowly

Commentary:
NOTE: may be used metaphorically

Examples:
He edged towards the car.
Giles edged the car forward and drawing closer we could see them.
At two in the afternoon I nervously edged in to Theory of Computation.
Slowly the moshing pit formed as they edged in to listen.
One hedgehog edged up to the hedge as the other hedgehog edged down.
The unemployment rate edged up to 5.8 percent in December.
