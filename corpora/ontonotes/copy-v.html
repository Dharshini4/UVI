copy-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: replicate in writing or a written document

Commentary: Includes COPY OUT, COPY DOWN
NOTE: Includes 'send a copy (of a written document to)' as in an email or a memo. See sentence 5.

Examples:
The students were made to copy the alphabet over and over again.
Copy the notes on the board.
Copy the information in the cell.
He copied the classified documents word for word.
Will you copy me on your response to the boss about annoying e-memos?

Mappings:
VerbNet: scribble-25.2,transcribe-25.4
FrameNet: Duplication
PropBank: copy.01,copy.02
WordNet 23.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 3
WordNet Verb Particle Constructions, Multiword Expressions:
copy_out 1

Sense Number 2: replicate an item or one's behavior/looks.

Commentary:
NOTE: Replicating ANY written document does not go in this sense. Tag as sense 1.

Examples:
She's copying me!
The mime copied the passers-by.
Children often copy their parents or older siblings.

FrameNet: Imitating,Duplication
