liberalize-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: Make liberal or more liberal, become more liberal, usually in terms of laws or rules.

Examples:
The laws liberalized after Prohibition.
The EC liberalized capital movements.
We have a conflict between the desire to liberalize economically
and to retain political control.
