unclothe-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: strip coverings from

Examples:
We should unclothe ourselves, so to speak, from life.
He had already unclothed his face of all show of malice.
He would tackle love songs clothed with sickly sentimentality and unclothe them
Unclothe your heart of envy.
She unclothed her innermost feelings.
