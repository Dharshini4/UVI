streamline-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: Contour economically or efficiently.

Examples:
They streamlined a 194-word document on government travel down to 45 words.
They streamlined the installation process and made it simple.
Nowadays companies are streamlining their workflows by converting
paper documents to digital forms.
Belgian food retailer Delhaize has streamlined its executive team in
the US.
