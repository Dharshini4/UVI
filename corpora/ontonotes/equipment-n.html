equipment-n; 4 Senses

Sense Number 1: physical items used in some context and purpose

Commentary: EQUIPMENT[+entity][+artifact][+item][+useful][+in_context] an instrumentality needed for an undertaking or to perform a service

Examples:
John spent $2000 on new office equipment.
This store sells all kinds of sports equipment.
A lot of heavy equipment was parked at the construction site.
A good cook needs the right equipment in the kitchen.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: the act or process of supplying necessary items or tools

Commentary: EQUIPMENT[+event][+supplying][+artifacts][+required][+for_purpose]
NOTE: X's equipment of Y -> X equipped Y (nominalization)

Examples:
The construction and equipment of a new marina will take some time.
His equipment of the boat for a long passage including stocking provisions, checking electronics and mending sails.
Mary's equipment of her new kitchen took months of decisions and thousands of dollars.

Mappings:
WordNet 0.0 Sense Numbers: 1a

Sense Number 3: inherent mental faculty, ability

Commentary: EQUIPMENT[+quality][+mental][+faculty][+ability]

Examples:
These students lack the intellectual equipment to learn such advanced types of mathematics.
She has the equipment to perform all the multitasking required to run an emergency ward.

Mappings:
WordNet 0.0 Sense Numbers: 1b

Sense Number 4: male genitalia, slang

Commentary: EQUIPMENT[+entity][+anatomical][+genitalia][+male]
NOTE: slang, vulgar usage

Examples:
The pornography magazines show men with exceptional equipment.
The ad claims this drug will enlarge your equipment considerably.

Mappings:
WordNet 0.0 Sense Numbers: 1c
