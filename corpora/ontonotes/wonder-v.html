wonder-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: express a desire to know; speculate about something; think or ponder

Examples:
Shouldn't you call home? Your parents must be wondering where you are.
He is starting to wonder whether he did the right thing in accepting the offer.
Will this turkey be enough for eight, I wonder.
I wonder if you could you help me carry these books?
I was wondering if you could give me more information about the book.

Sense Number 2: be surprised or amazed

Examples:
I wonder how beautiful the nature is. I love it!
He wondered at her composure in such a crisis.
He was behaving so badly at school today, I wonder he wasn't sent home.
I don't wonder that she burst into tears after the way you spoke to her.
I always wonder at your choices. Don't you ever think before you jump?
