autumn-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: fall

Commentary: AUTUMN[+event] the season when the leaves fall from the trees

Examples:
John likes autumn because he likes to play in the fallen leaves.
She graduated in the autumn of 1973.
Autumn is almost as colorful as spring.
We had a short autumn this year because the blizzards started in September.
