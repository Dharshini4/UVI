laminate-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Create a coating for something.

Examples:
We laminated the metal to make it suitable to coat the roof.
She laminated the thin sheets of wood together to make a veneer.
We must laminate the plastic.

Sense Number 2: Cover with a thin sheet of non-fabric material.

Examples:
We laminated the table to protect it.
Laminate the posters for hanging.
He should laminate the surface of the work bench.
