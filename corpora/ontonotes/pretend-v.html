pretend-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: behave unnaturally (to deceive), claim, feign or act.

Commentary: Syntax Includes: NP1[agent] PRETEND TO NP2 NP1[agent] PRETEND (that) NP2 NP1[agent] PRETEND NP2

Examples:

Let's pretend!
Never pretend to a love which you do not feel.
He pretended the title of King.
Melody pretended the role of predator in The Black Forest.
I cannot pretend to know the truth.
