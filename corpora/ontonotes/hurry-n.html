hurry-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: The state or condition of feeling or being rushed.

Commentary: HURRY[+movement][+event]

Examples:
I am in a hurry to leave work today.
In his hurry to leave he forgot his book.
We are in a hurry to finish the project.
