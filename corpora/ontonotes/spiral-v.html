spiral-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: Form a spiral or move in a spiral course.

Commentary: INCLUDES: SPIRAL UP, SPIRAL DOWN, SPIRAL INTO, SPIRAL AROUND

Examples:
Smoke from the fire spiraled into the sky.
The news sent UAL stock spiraling downward.
Inventories swelled throughout the distribution chain as prices
spiraled up.
