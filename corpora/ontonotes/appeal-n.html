appeal-n; 4 Senses

Sense Number 1: an entreaty, a request or solicitation

Commentary: APPEAL[+action][+request][+entreaty/+solicitation/+bid]
NOTE: can be spoken or written, or non-verbally expressed

Examples:
The parents' appeal to the kidnappers to release their child was shown on national television.
There was no appeal for forgiveness on the man's face.
She made an appeal to their sense of fair play in her summary to the jury.
There is a public appeal to raise $200,000 for the flood victims.
