thrum-v; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: sound with a monotonous hum

Commentary: Syntax includes: NP1[theme] THRUM NP2[location] NP1[theme] THRUM

Examples:
The song thrummed in the guttural basso of dwarves, as if to mock his concern and consternation.
The Mach-1's engine was thrumming in mechanical symphony.
The diesel engine thrummed.
The current thrummed the line.
I was standing in front of the power station drinking in the excess power that thrummed the air.
