honour-v; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: show respect for

Examples:
The group honoured the following doctors with awards at their International Symposium.
Today we honour our soldiers.
Today we had our puja and honoured the mountain Gods.
If we honoured the Wright Brothers' first flight with an elaborate Blue Angels air show,
would it humble their accomplishments?

Sense Number 2: accept as payment

Examples:
We honour checks and drafts.
Do you honour coupons online?

Sense Number 3: keep a promise, contract, or agreement

Examples:
Do you honour the postmark for delinquent payments?
We honour competitors' sale prices.
This pharmacy honours these insurance plans.
The government should protest when it thinks that another government is failing to honour treaty obligations.
China urges the United States to honour its words on the Taiwan issue.
She sacrificed her body and, ultimately, her soul to honour her promise.
