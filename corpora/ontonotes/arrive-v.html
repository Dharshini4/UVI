arrive-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: reach a destination or goal

Commentary: Syntax: NP1 ARRIVE (PP) Includes: ARRIVE AT
NOTE: Includes animate or inanimate subjects.

Examples:
On Mondays that train arrives at seven.
Wednesday arrived before we knew it.
With John's brilliant best-seller I would say he has arrived.
She is arriving at the same conclusion.
I wish they would arrive on time just once.
Two packages arrived yesterday.
