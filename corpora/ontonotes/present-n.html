present-n; 3 Senses

Sense Number 1: nowadays

Commentary: the period of time that is happening now; any continuous stretch of time including the moment of speech

Examples:
That is enough for the present.
He lives in the present with no thought of tomorrow.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: gift

Commentary: something presented as a gift

Examples:
His tie was a present from his wife.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 2

Sense Number 3: verb tense

Commentary: a verb tense that expresses actions or states at the time of speaking

Examples:
Beginners always learn to conjugate verbs into the present, at first.
Even the present tense conjugations can look strange for irregular verbs.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 3
