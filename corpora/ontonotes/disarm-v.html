disarm-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: Physically remove weaponry from.

Examples:
The U.S. is trying to get Russia to disarm.
You must disarm your opponent so that you hold the weapon.
We cannot simply tell American families to disarm; we must give them
a reason to do so.

Mappings:

WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1, 3

Sense Number 2: Make less emotionally hostile; win over mentally or emotionally.

Examples:
Her charm disarmed the prosecution lawyer completely.
Sometimes it's hard to disarm yourself on a first date.
