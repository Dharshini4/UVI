information-n; 4 Senses

Sense Number 1: data

Commentary: info, facts - knowledge acquired

Examples:
Statistical information indicates that Suzy is right.
You can find all of the information you need on the internet.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 2 3

Sense Number 2: message

Commentary: a message received and understood; noun product of verb "to inform"

Examples:
Information of his death was greatly exaggerated.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 3: entropy

Commentary: selective information - a numerical measure of the uncertainty of an outcome

Examples:
The signal contained thousands of bits of information.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 4

Sense Number 4: accusation

Commentary: INFORMATION[+event] act of informing - formal accusation of a crime; the act of informing the police about a crime

Examples:
Information would be greatly appreciated and informants will remain anonymous.
The information was by the grandmother.

Mappings:
WordNet 2.0 Sense Numbers: 5
