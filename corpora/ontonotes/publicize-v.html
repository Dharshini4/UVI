publicize-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: make something generally known, advertise

Examples:
The Senator publicized the same information about boarding passes six months ago.
Before the problem was publicized, the children from the contaminated school were dismissed.
Her battered face publicized the problem of domestic abuse.
Please don't publicize this blog to other members of the community.
