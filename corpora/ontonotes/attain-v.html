attain-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: Reach or hit.

Commentary: Syntax is: NP1 ATTAIN NP2

Examples:
He would not be satisfied until he had taught golf to roll over and
play dead, and he had attained the body fat percentage of a
rutabaga.
Then, you will have already attained one goal for today on the road
to success in life.
Once you've attained the desired location, make sure that
the projector is aligned horizontally straight.
Stirring was continued for 14 hours, while the cooling bath
attained room temperature.
The ecosystem attained equilibrium.
