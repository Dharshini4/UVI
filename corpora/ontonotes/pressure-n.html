pressure-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: physical force

Examples:
the compressed gas exerts an increased pressure
he used pressure to stop the bleeding
That tank is under high pressure.
I felt pressure on my face mask as I descended during the dive.
With novocaine, patients only experience pressure, not pain, during dental procedures.

Mappings:
WordNet 1.7 Sense Numbers: 1, 3, 5

Sense Number 2: social or emotional demands

Examples:
the public brought pressure to bear on the government
She felt constant pressure at her job.
The pressure on him to win the Gold Medal was enormous.

Mappings:
WordNet 1.7 Sense Numbers: 2, 4
