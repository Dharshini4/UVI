fend-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: manage or survive without assistance

Commentary: Includes: FEND FOR ONESELF

Examples:
The children are now old enough to fend for themselves.
He's used to fending for himself.
We had to fend for ourselves until we were rescued.
We have enough clothes to fend against the cold.
