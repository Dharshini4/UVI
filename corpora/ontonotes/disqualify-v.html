disqualify-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: declare or make unfit or unsuitable

Commentary: Syntax Is: NP1 DISQUALIFY NP2

Examples:
The United States was disqualified Monday from its fourth-place
finish in the Olympic team dressage event for a positive drug test.
Her pregnancy disqualified her from being able to lift 150 pounds.
