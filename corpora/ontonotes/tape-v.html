tape-v; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: fasten or attach with tape

Commentary: INCLUDES: TAPE UP

Examples:
Ashley taped his picture to the inside of her locker door.
They will tape up your pants (for guys) if undies are showing.

Mappings:
VerbNet: tape-22.4
FrameNet: Attaching
PropBank: tape.01
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Sense Number 2: record audio or video on magnetic tape or electronically

Commentary: Includes: TAPE RECORD

Examples:
The president taped his radio address this morning.
She tapes 'All My Children' while she's at work and then watches it when she gets home.
He taped more than 19 video entries for 'Primetime.'
