storey-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: one level of rooms in a multi-floored building

Commentary: STOREY[+structure][+architectural][+level][+of_building][+multi_floored]
NOTE: variant spelling of 'story' meaning the same thing (NOT a tale)

Examples:

His penthouse is on the eight storey, which is the top floor of the apartment building.
