hurl-v; 1 Senses

suspected militants hurled grenades into a mosque in northwest
Pakistan during Wednesday evening prayers, an official said.
He hurled at Aias with his bright spear.
He laughed, hurled insults and kicked me as I lay on the street sobbing.
