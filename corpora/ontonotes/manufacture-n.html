manufacture-n; 4 Senses

Sense Number 1: manmade fabrication of goods

Commentary: MANUFACTURE[+process][+fabrication][+manmade][+of_goods]

Examples:

The manufacture of lead_based paint is highly regulated.
The factory was reconfigured for the manufacture of paper products.
