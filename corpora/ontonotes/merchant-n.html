merchant-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: commercially-engaged person or group

Commentary: Refers to any person engaging in mercantile activities. Not an adjective such as 'merchant class' or 'merchant marine'.

Examples:
The agora was full of cloth merchants.
The friendly merchant sold me a bottle for a hundred rupees.
