quip-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: make a witty remark or joke

Examples:
The conversation ended when he quipped in a strange groggy voice, 'I think I'm in love with her.'
He quipped while Napoleon quaked.
He quipped that the shooting had ruined his favorite suit.
Legislators did not debate, but they quipped that this is the closest they've come to harmony.
