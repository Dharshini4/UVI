print-n; 8 Senses

Sense Number 1: written typographical text in a document

Commentary: PRINT[+entity][+physical][+form][+written][+typographical][+text]

Examples:
The print at the bottom of the page is too small to read easily.
The heading of each section in the paper was in bold print.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 1

Commentary: PRINT[+entity][+physical][+artifact][+reproduction][+from_master][+picture][+artwork]

Examples:

A framed print of Van Gogh's painting 'Sunflowers' hung over Sally's couch.

Mappings:
WordNet 3.0 Sense Numbers: 2

Sense Number 3: a visible impression on a physical surface

Commentary: PRINT[+entity][+physical][+form][+impression][+visible][+surface]
