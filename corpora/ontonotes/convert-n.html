convert-n; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: a person who has joined a religion or political cause they did not formerly belong to

Commentary: CONVERT[+person][+role][+member][+new][+of_belief_system]

Examples:
The poet became a convert to Catholicism late in life.
These voters are recent converts to the Libertarian Party.
After trying out Mary's Mac, John became an instant convert. (He preferred her computer to the one he'd been using before)
