blockade-v; 1 Senses

Sense Number 1: block a passageway or access, blockade

Commentary: Syntax Includes: NP1[agent] BLOCKADE NP2[patient[object]] Np1[agent] BLOCKADE NP2[location]

Examples:
He blockaded her vehicle in his reserved spot.
There he blockaded her till near Christmas, when she escaped, and
the castle surrendered.
The price of wheat soared when the French blockaded ships carrying
supplies to Britian.
About sixty thousand people blockaded the downtown streets.
