compliance-n; 2 Senses

Sense Number 1: The act of behaving according to accepted standards.

Commentary: COMPLIANCE[+event]

Examples:
Their financial statements are generally in compliance with
regulations.
He behaved in compliance with her every whim.

Sense Number 2: A disposition to yield to the will of others.

Commentary: COMPLIANCE[+disposition]

Examples:
Her compliance was a prerequisite for their traditional marriage.
A dog's compliance is an important part of the relationship.
